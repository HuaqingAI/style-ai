## Common Issues & Fixes

### Issue: Wordmark Too Small

**Symptom**: The horizontal SVG lockup is too small to read on mobile.  
**Cause**: The source wordmark is wide.  
**Fix**: Use symbol-only SVG plus visible text `Style AI`.

### Issue: Header Too Tall

**Symptom**: Login panel is pushed too low on iPhone SE.  
**Cause**: Main headline or spacing is too large.  
**Fix**: Reduce headline size to the compact mobile range while keeping hierarchy clear.

### Issue: Brand Accent Too Strong

**Symptom**: Purple reads as the dominant UI color.  
**Cause**: Overuse of spark purple.  
**Fix**: Keep purple limited to the symbol spark, small label, or one subtle accent.

## Issue Log

### 2026-05-13 - Brand Symbol Blended Into Header Background

**Problem**: The copied symbol SVG used a deep black app-tile background. On the deep black login header, the symbol tile blended into the surface and only the purple spark remained visible.

**Root Cause**: The story allowed symbol-only SVG usage, but did not require contrast treatment when the asset itself contains a black tile and is placed on a black product surface.

**Solution**: Wrapped the symbol in a compact warm-off-white badge with an 8px radius, subtle border, and small padding. The SVG geometry, S path, spark, and colors were not edited.

**Code Change**: Updated `01.1-start-login.html` so the brand image sits inside a visible badge container instead of directly on the dark header.

**Learned**: When placing `icn-001` on dark login surfaces, provide a contrasting badge or use a transparent adaptive foreground asset; do not place the black app-tile SVG directly on black UI.
