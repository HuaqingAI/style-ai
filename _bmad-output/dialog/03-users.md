# Step 07: Target Users

## Opening Question

Saga asked what the first seed users look like behaviorally: whether they browse Xiaohongshu/Douyin, shop online, have strong beauty motivation, tend to struggle with hairstyles or outfits, and whether they are willing to upload personal photos.

## Key Exchanges

- Sue noted that some people may be unwilling to upload photos, but most target users are willing to try. Those unwilling to upload are not target users for MVP.
- Saga identified willingness to upload photos or build a personal profile as a qualifying condition.
- Sue said the relevant psychological states vary by scenario: wanting to become better-looking, seeing an outfit/hairstyle but fearing it will fail, dissatisfaction with current image, or wanting to try a new style without real-world risk.
- Saga summarized the shared underlying need: users cannot imagine "what this will look like on me" and need a credible, specific, low-pressure basis for judgment.
- Sue confirmed the primary user is someone actively motivated to improve appearance but lacking judgment.
- Sue selected shopping-decision users and light curiosity users as secondary user groups.

## Primary User Definition

**主动想变美但缺少判断力的新手**

他们愿意上传照片或建立个人档案，对发型、穿搭、妆容、配饰变化有兴趣，但不知道什么适合自己。触发点可能很多：想变好看、看到某个风格心动、对当前形象不满意、怕真实尝试翻车。核心痛点是想象不出来“放到我身上会怎样”，需要低成本、低压力、可信的试验方式。

## Secondary Users

### 购物决策型用户

他们在买衣服、配饰、尝试发型服务前使用 Style AI，目标是避免买错、剪错、烫错、搭错。对他们来说，产品价值是“购买/改变前先看自己效果”。

### 轻度好奇型用户

他们没有强烈变美任务，但愿意用免费额度玩一玩，看自己不同风格下的样子。如果生成效果足够惊喜，可能转为付费或分享。

## Explicit Non-Target Users

- 强隐私顾虑、不愿上传照片或建立个人形象档案的人。
- 需要专业造型师级深度服务、人工咨询或 B 端商家工具的人。
