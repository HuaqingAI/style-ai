# Step 11: Tone of Voice

## Context

Style AI serves daily beauty beginners who want to see realistic versions of themselves in different hairstyles, outfits, makeup, and accessories. Users may feel uncertain or sensitive when receiving appearance-related feedback, so UI microcopy must be helpful without judging or pressuring.

## Suggested Tone Attributes

1. **温和但不讨好**: Avoid harsh appearance judgment while staying useful.
2. **具体但不专业压迫**: Give clear next steps without excessive styling jargon.
3. **鼓励尝试但保留选择权**: Recommend confidently, but keep the user's final preference respected.
4. **可信且克制**: Avoid exaggerated transformation promises.
5. **轻松生活化**: Feel like a daily beauty tool, not a professional beauty exam.

## Example Microcopy

**Error Message:**
- Generic: "图片不合格"
- Style AI: "这张照片有点难判断，换一张光线更清楚的试试。"

**Loading Message:**
- Generic: "Processing request"
- Style AI: "正在生成你的风格效果，稍等一下。"

**Recommendation:**
- Generic: "最佳发型：中发层次"
- Style AI: "这个方向比较稳：中发层次会更修饰脸型，也保留自然感。"

**Avoid Guidance:**
- Generic: "不适合：厚重齐刘海"
- Style AI: "这个造型可以先谨慎尝试：厚重齐刘海可能会让整体显得更闷。"

**Button Text:**
- Generic: "Submit"
- Style AI: "开始看看效果"

**Profile Save Prompt:**
- Generic: "是否保存个人档案？"
- Style AI: "保存这张作为常用形象？下次生成可以少传一次。"

## Do

- Use clear, gentle, practical phrasing.
- Explain why a style is recommended or risky.
- Keep the user's choice open.
- Use everyday Chinese rather than professional-heavy beauty terms.
- Make privacy and saving behavior explicit.

## Don't

- Use appearance-shaming words such as "显老", "土", "丑", "脸大" as direct labels.
- Use exaggerated marketing claims such as "一键逆袭" or "颜值暴涨".
- Present AI output as the only correct answer.
- Use vague motivational filler that does not help the user decide.
