## Confirmed MVP Success Criteria

**Testing Scale:** 20-50 seed users

**Testing Timeline:** 7-14 days

**Primary Metric:** At least 60% of users complete a first personal image generation or try-on generation and say the result "looks like me" and is useful for judging fit/style.

**Secondary Metrics:**
- At least 30% of users perform a second generation after the first result.
- At least 10%-20% of users buy generation credits or explicitly say they are willing to pay for more generations.
- At least 5 users give concrete feedback such as "I would use this to judge whether a hairstyle/clothing/accessory suits me."
- Critical quality problems such as failed generation, not looking like the user, or style drift are recorded for the next iteration.

**North Star for MVP Validation:** Whether seed users, after seeing their own generated result, are willing to keep spending credits to explore more styles.
