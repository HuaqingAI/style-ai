# Step 02: Vision

## Opening Question

Saga asked what change Sue most wanted a "日常变美的新手" to experience after using Style AI.

## User Signals

- Sue wanted users to "更知道自己适合什么".
- The desired outcome is not that AI gives one final answer, but that users build judgment about their own style.
- Sue provided a hairstyle analysis example showing realistic previews of mainstream hairstyles, recommendations, avoid items, comparisons, and explanatory advice.
- The product should show realistic versions of the user under different mainstream styles, then give analysis and suggestions; final choice remains personal preference.

## Reflection Checkpoint

Saga reflected the vision as:

Style AI wants to become a personal style lab for daily beauty beginners: users can see realistic effects of different mainstream styles before actually perming hair, buying clothes, applying makeup, or changing their look, while receiving clear fit analysis. It lowers experimentation cost, helps users gradually understand what suits them, and preserves personal taste and final choice.

Sue confirmed: OK.

## Final Vision Statement

Style AI 是日常变美新手的个人风格试验室：用户不用真的烫头、买衣服、化妆或换造型，就能先看到自己在不同主流风格下的逼真效果，并获得清晰的适配分析。

它的目标不是替用户规定唯一正确答案，而是降低尝试成本，帮助用户逐渐知道自己适合什么，同时保留个人喜好和最终选择权。

## Key Insights

- 产品价值从“抽象建议”升级为“真实预览 + 适配分析 + 自主选择”。
- 新手用户需要先看到自己真实效果，才能降低尝试新风格的心理门槛。
- 推荐与避坑需要解释原因，但不能压倒个人喜好。
- 发型、穿搭、妆容、配饰都可以采用“主流风格枚举 + 自己真实预览 + 推荐/不推荐 + 原因”的框架。
